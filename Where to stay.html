Best places to stay in NYC!

Hotel Pennsylvania

This Manhattan hotel is across the street from Madison Square Garden and Penn Station. The hotel offers a gym and a tour desk with ticket services.401 7th Avenue, New York City, NY

Park Central

Just under a mile from Central Park and Times Square, this modern Manhattan hotel features completely renovated rooms, equipped with amenities including a flat-screen TV. 870 7th Avenue at 56th Street, New York City, NY

Row NYC

In the Theater District and within a 3-minute walk of Times Square, this hotel features a fitness center and a concierge service. Rockefeller Center is a short walk away. 700 8th Avenue, New York City, NY

New York Marriott Marquis

In the heart of Times Square and the Broadway theater district, this 4-star hotel features 3 dining options, including a revolving rooftop restaurant. 1535 Broadway, New York City, NY

Hudson, A Morgans Original

Featuring an interior lobby garden with trees and ivy walls, this boutique hotel is within a 5-minute walk of Central Park and the Columbus Circle subway station. 356 West 58th Street, New York City, NY

Park Lane Hotel

The Park Lane Hotel is a deluxe hotel with a European atmosphere, providing views of Central Park and the New York skyline. 36 Central Park South, New York City, NY

Wyndham New Yorker Hotel

This historic Midtown Manhattan hotel is a 2-minute walk from Madison Square Garden and across from Penn Station. 481 Eighth Avenue, New York City, NY

New York Hilton Midtown

In the center of midtown Manhattan, this New York hotel within a 10-minute walk of both Central Park and Times Square. 1335 Avenue of Americas, New York City, NY

Waldorf Astoria New York

The Waldorf Astoria, a landmark hotel on Park Avenue in Manhattan, offers luxurious rooms with marble bathrooms, just blocks from upscale shopping on 5th Avenue. 301 Park Avenue, New York City, NY

Fairfield Inn by Marriott Times Square

In downtown Manhattan and just a 5-minute walk from Times Square, this modern hotel features a rooftop bar and restaurant overlooking the city. Penn Station is a 10-minute walk away. 330 West 40th Street, New York City, NY

Crowne Plaza Times Square

In Times Square, this Manhattan hotel is within a 7 minute walk of Rockefeller Center and Radio City Music Hall. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a concierge service and on-site dining. 1605 Broadway Avenue, New York City, NY

InterContinental New York Times Square

Towering 36 stories above the city, the Intercontinental Times Square provides guests with front row seats to stunning views of New Yorkâ€™s impressive skyline and the Hudson River. 300 West 44th Street, New York City, NY

Time Hotel

This boutique hotel in midtown Manhattan's theater district offers modern rooms and an Italian restaurant. It's next to Times Square and 2 blocks from Rockefeller Center. 224 West 49th Street, New York City, NY

Days Hotel Broadway at 94th Street

On the Upper West Side of Manhattan and within walking distance of Central Park, this hotel features an on-site fitness room and accommodations with cable TV. 215 West 94th Street, New York City, NY
